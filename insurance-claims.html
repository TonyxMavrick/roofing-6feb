Insurance Claims FAQs

Will my insurance cover roof damage?

Most homeowners insurance policies cover sudden and accidental damage from storms, wind, hail, and falling objects. Coverage depends on your specific policy and the cause of damage.

How long do I have to file a claim?

Most insurance policies require claims within 1-2 years of damage, but it's best to file as soon as possible. Prompt filing helps ensure better documentation and faster processing.

Do you charge for insurance claim help?

Our insurance claims assistance is included when you choose us for your roofing repairs. We only get paid when your project is completed successfully.

What if my claim is denied?

We can help you appeal denied claims with additional documentation, expert assessments, and professional negotiation to get your claim reconsidered.
