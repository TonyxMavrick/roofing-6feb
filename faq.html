General Questions

How quickly can you respond to emergency roof repairs?

We offer 24/7 emergency roofing services and can typically respond within 2-4 hours depending on your location and weather conditions. Our emergency team is always on standby to help with urgent roofing issues that can't wait.

Do you offer free inspections?

Yes! We provide completely free, no-obligation roof inspections for all potential customers. Our certified professionals will conduct a thorough assessment and provide you with a detailed report including photos and recommendations.

Are you licensed and insured?

Absolutely. PrimeShield Roofing is fully licensed, bonded, and insured with comprehensive liability coverage. We maintain all required state and local licenses and can provide proof of insurance upon request.

What areas do you serve?

We provide professional roofing services across all major US cities and states. Our nationwide network of certified professionals ensures consistent quality service wherever you are located. See our full service area.

Pricing & Estimates

How much does roof repair cost?

Roof repair costs vary depending on the extent of damage, materials needed, and roof accessibility. Minor repairs typically range from $300-$1,500, while major repairs can cost $1,500-$7,000. We provide free detailed estimates with transparent pricing and no hidden fees.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing options to make roof repairs and replacements more affordable. Contact us to discuss payment plans that work within your budget.

Do you provide written estimates?

Yes, all estimates are provided in detailed written form with itemized costs, materials specifications, and project timeline. We believe in complete transparency with no surprise charges.

Insurance & Warranties

Will my homeowners insurance cover roof damage?

Most homeowners insurance policies cover sudden and accidental roof damage from storms, wind, hail, and falling objects. However, coverage depends on your specific policy and the cause of damage. We provide comprehensive documentation and work directly with insurance companies to help maximize your claim.

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes! We provide complete insurance claims assistance including damage documentation, meeting with adjusters, filing paperwork, and negotiating fair settlements. Our experience helps ensure you receive the coverage you're entitled to. Learn more about our insurance assistance.

What warranties do you offer?

We provide comprehensive warranties covering both materials and workmanship. Material warranties vary by manufacturer (typically 20-50 years), and our workmanship warranty covers our installation for a minimum of 10 years. All warranty terms are clearly outlined in your contract.

Roof Maintenance & Lifespan

How long does a roof typically last?

Roof lifespan varies by material: Asphalt shingles (15-30 years), Metal roofing (40-70 years), Tile roofing (50-100 years), and Slate roofing (75-200 years). Proper maintenance, climate, and installation quality all affect longevity.

How often should I have my roof inspected?

We recommend professional roof inspections at least twice a year (spring and fall) and after any major weather events. Regular inspections help identify small issues before they become expensive repairs.

Can I repair my roof myself?

While minor tasks like cleaning gutters can be DIY, most roof repairs require professional expertise and safety equipment. Improper repairs can void warranties, create safety hazards, and lead to more expensive damage. We always recommend professional assessment and repair.

What are signs that my roof needs repair?

Common warning signs include: water stains on ceilings, missing or damaged shingles, granules in gutters, sagging roof deck, increased energy bills, visible light through roof boards, and moss or algae growth. If you notice any of these, schedule a free inspection immediately.
