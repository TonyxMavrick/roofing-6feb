Top Signs Your Roof Needs Immediate Repair

üìÖ January 15, 2026 ‚úçÔ∏è Mike Thompson üìñ 5 min read üè∑Ô∏è Roof Care

Your roof is your home's first line of defense against the elements. While roofs are built to last, they don't last forever. Knowing when your roof needs professional attention can save you thousands of dollars in water damage repairs and prevent small issues from becoming major problems. Here are the top warning signs that indicate your roof needs immediate repair.

1. Water Stains on Ceilings or Walls

Brown or yellow stains on your ceiling or walls are clear indicators of water intrusion from your roof. These stains often appear after heavy rain and can indicate active leaks that require immediate attention. Don't ignore even small stains ‚Äì they can signal significant underlying damage.

2. Missing or Damaged Shingles

Missing shingles create gaps in your roof's protection, allowing water to seep into the underlayment and roof deck. Check your roof after storms for missing, cracked, curled, or buckled shingles. Even a few damaged shingles can lead to leaks if not addressed promptly.

3. Granules in Gutters

Asphalt shingles are coated with protective granules that guard against UV damage. As shingles age and deteriorate, they shed these granules. If you notice significant granule accumulation in your gutters or downspouts, your shingles are aging and may need replacement soon.

4. Sagging Roof Deck

A sagging or drooping roof deck indicates serious structural damage, often from prolonged water exposure. This is an emergency situation requiring immediate professional assessment. A sagging roof can eventually collapse if not addressed.

5. Increased Energy Bills

If your heating or cooling bills have increased without explanation, your roof might be the culprit. Poor ventilation or roof damage can compromise your home's insulation, forcing HVAC systems to work harder. A professional roof inspection can identify insulation issues.

6. Daylight Through Roof Boards

If you can see daylight through your roof boards when you're in the attic, you have holes that need immediate repair. Where light gets in, water will follow. This is a clear sign that professional roof repair is needed immediately.

7. Mold or Moss Growth

While some moss growth is aesthetic, excessive mold, moss, or algae can indicate moisture problems and trapped debris. These organisms can hold moisture against your roof, accelerating deterioration and potentially causing rot in the roof deck.

What to Do Next

If you've noticed any of these warning signs, don't wait to address them. Schedule a professional roof inspection immediately. Early intervention can often mean the difference between a simple repair and a costly replacement.
