Signs Your Roof May Have Storm Damage

Visible Exterior Signs

  • Missing or damaged shingles
  • Dents or cracks in roofing material
  • Granule loss in gutters
  • Damaged flashing around vents/chimneys
  • Debris on roof

Interior Warning Signs

  • Water stains on ceilings/walls
  • Dripping or moisture in attic
  • Musty odors
  • Peeling paint or wallpaper
  • Increased energy bills
